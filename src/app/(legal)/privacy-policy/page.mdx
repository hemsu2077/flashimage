# Privacy Policy for FlashImage

**Effective Date**: July 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to FlashImage (picturetodrawing.com), an AI-powered service that transforms your photos into beautiful drawings and artwork. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

## Information Collection and Use

We collect the following types of data to provide you with a better experience while using FlashImage:

### 1. Account Information

- **What We Collect**: Your name, email address, and any other information you provide when creating an account.
- **Purpose**: To manage your account, provide customer support, and enable access to our core features.
- **Note**: Account registration is required to use our core image transformation features.

### 2. Image Data

- **What We Collect**: Images you upload to our platform for processing and transformation.
- **Purpose**: To provide AI-powered image-to-drawing conversion services.
- **Data Retention**: Uploaded images are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours after processing for your privacy and security.
- **Privacy Guarantee**: Your uploaded images are never shared with third parties and are processed solely for the purpose of generating your requested artwork.

### 3. Usage Analytics

- **What We Collect**: We use Google Analytics and similar analytics tools to collect information about how you interact with our website, including pages visited, features used, time spent, and general usage patterns.
- **Purpose**: To analyze user engagement, improve our services, optimize user experience, and understand how our platform is being used.
- **Data Type**: This includes anonymized and aggregated data that cannot be used to personally identify you.

### 4. Device and Technical Information

- **What We Collect**: Data regarding the devices you use to access FlashImage, such as device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, and screen resolution.
- **Purpose**: To optimize our services for different devices, ensure compatibility, and provide technical support.

### 5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

- **What We Collect**: Small data files placed on your device that help us track user preferences, maintain login sessions, and improve user experience.
- **Purpose**: To enhance the functionality of our services, personalize your experience, and support our analytics efforts.

### 6. Payment Information

- **What We Collect**: Payment processing is handled securely by Stripe, our trusted payment processor. We do not store your complete credit card information on our servers.
- **Stripe Integration**: Stripe collects and processes your payment information including credit card details, billing address, and transaction data in accordance with PCI DSS standards.
- **What We Store**: We only store transaction IDs, payment status, and billing information necessary for account management and customer support.
- **Purpose**: To facilitate secure billing and payment processing for our services.

## Data Storage and Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement multiple layers of protection:

- **Encryption**: All data transmitted to and from our servers is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
- **Access Controls**: Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access user data.
- **Image Security**: Uploaded images are processed in a secure environment and automatically deleted within 24 hours.
- **Payment Security**: All payment processing is handled by Stripe, which maintains PCI DSS Level 1 compliance, the highest level of security certification.
- **Server Security**: Our servers are hosted in secure data centers with physical and network security measures.

However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

- **Legal Compliance**: To comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests from public authorities.
- **Safety and Security**: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our users or others.
- **Service Providers**: With trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our website (such as Stripe for payment processing), subject to strict confidentiality agreements.
- **Analytics**: Anonymized and aggregated data may be shared with analytics providers like Google Analytics to help us improve our services.

**Important**: Your uploaded images are never shared with any third parties and are processed solely for providing you with the requested artwork.

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

- **Google Analytics**: For website usage analytics and user behavior analysis
- **Stripe**: For secure payment processing and subscription management
- **Authentication Services**: For secure user login and account management

Each of these services has their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access**: Request access to the personal information we hold about you
- **Correction**: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal obligations)
- **Data Portability**: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
- **Opt-out**: Opt out of non-essential cookies and analytics tracking

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hi@picturetodrawing.com.

## Data Retention

- **Account Information**: Retained for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services
- **Images**: Automatically deleted within 24 hours after processing
- **Payment Information**: Retained as required for tax and legal purposes
- **Analytics Data**: Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement
